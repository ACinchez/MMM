About Us

Might-Mite Motors, Inc. (MMM) is a closely held corporation, established in 1980, that manufactures and markets miniature ridable motor vehicles for children. Our products include several models of cars, trucks, all-terrain vehicles, and trains. All of our vehicles are powered by car batteries and can achieve speeds of up to 5 mph.

At this time, MMM is organized into three divisions: Product Development, Manufacturing, and Marketing and Sales. Each division is headed by a vice president, who reports directly to the CEO. All of these divisions are housed in a single location that the corporation owns outright. This allows us to maintain a tight-knit team and ensure the highest level of quality control throughout our entire manufacturing process.
